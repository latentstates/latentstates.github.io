Altered Reflections

An AI mirror that reimagines your face in real time. Visitors stood before a screen and watched as a neural network transformed their reflection — ageing, stylizing, fracturing their likeness into something familiar yet alien. A bridge between the science of self-perception and the art of generative imagery.

The Experience

A live camera feed processed through a real-time AI model that morphed the visitor’s face. The transformations ranged from subtle — age progression, gender shifts — to surreal: artistic style transfers, fragmentation. Each visitor saw themselves differently. The AI’s interpretation was unique each time.

The Bridge

Altered Reflections appeared at both Metamersion III (the art exhibition at Champalimaud) and LSC 2024 (the science camp at Waking Life). In the art context, it was an encounter with AI as creative mirror. In the science context, it generated data about self-perception and emotional response to AI-generated imagery. Same piece, two frameworks — the bridge between States and Spaces.
