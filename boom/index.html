Boom Festival

Where it started
2016, 2018 · Idanha-a-Nova, Portugal

Boom is a biennial transformational arts festival in Idanha-a-Nova, Portugal. In 2016, we brought a hundred wireless motion sensors to the dance floor and built Psiloscope — the first Latent States project. Boom was where the practice began: the conviction that perception and collective experience could be studied not in spite of the festival context but because of it.
